A little bit about me

I have a passion for learning new things and enjoy being challenged often. I first discovered programming during the height of the COVID-19 pandemic, searching for a new skill I could learn from home. I quickly fell in love and now hope to create a career that will allow me to use coding as an outlet for my creativity.

The oldest of five children, I was born in Florida and raised between Minnesota and Texas. I live in Dallas, Texas with my tiny family consisting of my girlfriend, Amy, and our dog (Sophie) and cat (Mira). My hobbies outside of coding are traveling, reading, cooking, and hiking.
